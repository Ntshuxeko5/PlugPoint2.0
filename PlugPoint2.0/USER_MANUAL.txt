===============================================================================
                                1. GETTING STARTED
===============================================================================

REGISTRATION
------------
1. Visit the homepage and click "Register" or "Sign Up"
2. Fill in your details:
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Password
   - Choose your role (Buyer, Seller, or Admin)
3. Click "Register" to create your account
4. You'll be automatically logged in and redirected to your dashboard

LOGIN
-----
1. Click "Login" in the navigation bar
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click "Login" to access your account

USER ROLES
----------
- BUYER: Can browse, purchase, review products, and message sellers
- SELLER: Can list products, manage orders, and communicate with buyers
- ADMIN: Can manage users, products, orders, and view system analytics

===============================================================================
                                2. BUYER GUIDE
===============================================================================

BROWSING PRODUCTS
-----------------
1. Visit the homepage to see featured products
2. Click "Products" in the navigation to browse all products
3. Use the search bar to find specific items
4. Filter products by:
   - Category (Electronics, Fashion, etc.)
   - Price range
   - Rating (1-5 stars)
   - Seller
5. Sort products by:
   - Newest first
   - Price (low to high or high to low)
   - Highest rated

SEARCH AND FILTERING
--------------------
Advanced Search Options:
- Search by product name or description
- Filter by category (Electronics, Fashion, Home & Garden, etc.)
- Set minimum and maximum price
- Filter by seller name
- Filter by rating (4+ stars, 3+ stars, etc.)
- Sort results by various criteria

SHOPPING CART
-------------
1. Click "Add to Cart" on any product
2. View your cart by clicking the cart icon in the navigation
3. In your cart, you can:
   - Review items and prices
   - Remove items you don't want
   - See the total cost
   - Proceed to checkout

WISHLIST
--------
1. Click "Add to Wishlist" on products you like
2. View your wishlist by clicking the heart icon
3. Remove items from wishlist when needed
4. Move items from wishlist to cart when ready to buy

MAKING PURCHASES
----------------
1. Add items to your cart
2. Click "Proceed to Checkout"
3. Review your order details
4. Complete payment through Paystack
5. You'll receive confirmation of your order
6. Track your order status in your dashboard

ORDER TRACKING
--------------
1. Click "Orders" in your dashboard
2. View all your past and current orders
3. Check order status:
   - Pending: Payment being processed
   - Paid: Payment confirmed
   - Shipped: Order dispatched
   - Completed: Order delivered
   - Cancelled: Order cancelled

PRODUCT REVIEWS
---------------
1. After purchasing a product, you can leave a review
2. Rate the product from 1-5 stars
3. Write a detailed review about your experience
4. Reviews help other buyers make informed decisions
5. You can only review products you've purchased

MESSAGING SELLERS
-----------------
1. Click "Message Seller" on any product page
2. Or access messages through your dashboard
3. Send questions about products before buying
4. Communicate about orders after purchase
5. View message history and mark as read

PROFILE MANAGEMENT
------------------
1. Click your profile picture/name in the navigation
2. Update your personal information:
   - Name
   - Phone number
   - Address
   - Profile picture
3. Change your password if needed
4. View your order history and statistics

===============================================================================
                                3. SELLER GUIDE
===============================================================================

SUBSCRIPTION PLANS
------------------
Before listing products, you need an active subscription:

BASIC PLAN (R299/month)
- List up to 10 products
- Basic analytics
- Customer support

PRO PLAN (R599/month)
- List up to 50 products
- Advanced analytics
- Priority support
- Featured product placement

ENTERPRISE PLAN (R999/month)
- Unlimited products
- Full analytics suite
- Premium support
- Custom branding options

To subscribe:
1. Go to "Subscription" in your dashboard
2. Choose your preferred plan
3. Complete payment through Paystack
4. Start listing products immediately

ADDING PRODUCTS
---------------
1. Ensure you have an active subscription
2. Click "Add Product" in your dashboard
3. Fill in product details:
   - Product name
   - Description
   - Price (in ZAR)
   - Category
   - Product image (JPG, PNG, GIF)
4. Click "Add Product" to submit
5. Products require admin approval before going live

MANAGING PRODUCTS
-----------------
1. View all your products in your dashboard
2. Check product status:
   - Pending: Awaiting admin approval
   - Active: Available for purchase
   - Rejected: Not approved (check admin feedback)
3. Edit product details if needed
4. Remove products that are no longer available

ORDER MANAGEMENT
----------------
1. View incoming orders in your dashboard
2. Order details include:
   - Buyer information
   - Product details
   - Order amount
   - Order status
3. Update order status as you process orders
4. Communicate with buyers about their orders

MESSAGING BUYERS
----------------
1. Respond to buyer inquiries through the messaging system
2. Send updates about orders
3. Answer questions about your products
4. Build relationships with customers

ANALYTICS DASHBOARD
-------------------
Your seller dashboard shows:
- Total products listed
- Active vs pending products
- Total sales revenue
- Number of orders
- Average product rating
- Recent orders
- Top selling products
- Monthly sales chart

PROFILE MANAGEMENT
------------------
1. Update your seller profile information
2. Add business details
3. Upload a professional profile picture
4. Set up contact information for customers

                                5. TROUBLESHOOTING
===============================================================================

COMMON ISSUES AND SOLUTIONS
---------------------------

CAN'T LOG IN?
- Check your email and password
- Ensure caps lock is off
- Try resetting your password
- Contact support if account is locked

PAYMENT NOT PROCESSING?
- Check your internet connection
- Ensure Paystack is properly configured
- Verify payment details are correct
- Contact support for payment issues

PRODUCT NOT APPEARING?
- Check if product is approved by admin
- Verify subscription is active
- Ensure all required fields are filled
- Contact admin for approval status

ORDER STATUS NOT UPDATING?
- Allow time for processing
- Check seller communication
- Contact seller directly
- Escalate to admin if needed

CAN'T UPLOAD IMAGES?
- Check file size (max 5MB)
- Ensure file type is supported (JPG, PNG, GIF)
- Verify directory permissions
- Contact support for technical issues

MESSAGING NOT WORKING?
- Check internet connection
- Refresh the page
- Clear browser cache
- Contact support if persistent
